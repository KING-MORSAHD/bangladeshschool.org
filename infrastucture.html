school infrastructure:

Bangladesh School bahrain and college is situated in the kanoo garden manama, Bahrain near the Quality Education School and Middle east school. Our school consists of many villas according to education class levels like for preschool, juniors,seniors and college. School follows the Dhaka board format and also IGCSE.The board exams (PSC, JSC,SSC and HSC) are conducted by the Dhaka board. Our school consists of 10 villas approximately, computer lab, science lab , library and a playground.



School Library:

School has one library room where students can pick any kinds of books which they want. There are different kinds of books available. There are rows of books which are seperated according to class levels. The library consists story books, knowledge books, religious stories book, science books, geographic books and many more.There are sitting arrangements available and all classes are given individuals periods to come and read books.

Our school has a computer lab where students are given individuals computer to do their work.All classes are given individuals periods to come and use the computer Lab. There are computer teachers and they r instructing students how to Use and works on computers like microsoft words,programming, powerpoint etc. There is a computer club and they conduct many competitons regarding the talents and experience of the students.

The Examination Department:

The Bangladesh School and College Examination cell plays a key role in conducting the internal examinations of the School like Periodic test, Term Exams, the Model / Mock exams for the Senior Classes and also the Annual Exams. There is sufficient number of supporting staff to supervise the routine work of the cell. The cell is under the direct supervision of the Principal.



Nurse Room:

Bangladesh School and College bahrain consists of a nurse room where students are treated if they get hurt and injuries. Theres are possibility of students getting injured during playing.Theirs a nurse who gives fastest treatment to the students and quick response.



Counselling Department:

The Guidance and Counselling department (GCD) of our school was established to face new psychological and social challenges amongst young learners that require guidance and counselling. The Department ensures that the school facilities which are in place are effectively utilized for the total development of the individual student, to enable the individual to lead a more fulfilling life. Students are guided not only in the discipline and behavior aspect but also to for the selection of subjects for further studies, career avenues, personal and social problems. The unit also coordinates the training and development programs with health and family life education in schools. The department has two academic counsellors / Social Counsellors who collaborate and facilitate with the individual to change in order to empower students to embrace their potential and achieve their personal and academic aspirations. The GCD is equipped with a counselling corner and a career guidance corner for 1 to 1 meetings with students. The department computers are LAN and Wi-Fi technology enabled. The GCD premises are CCTV monitored, well-furnished and is closely located to the principal and vice principal's offices for their advisory support and any immediate interventions.
