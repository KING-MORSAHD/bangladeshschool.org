About Us



Bangaldesh School Bahrain established in 1995 is one of the leading SSC Schools in the Gulf with classes from Kindergarten to Senior Secondary and more than 900 students of different nationalities by imparting education of international standards.



The School is under Bangladesh Embassy OF Bahrain, and this main power are going to embassy chairmans.



Excellence is the prime endeavour and striving towards it, is the main aim of the School. The deep passion and desire to make a difference in the lives of the children continues.



The academic atmosphere is developed in spacious well-equipped classrooms, a comprehensive library and reading room and laboratories for science and computer. The teaching faculty, which is not highly qualified and experienced, but having approach towards imparting knowledge and the teachers update their skills through in-service training programmes, workshops and seminars conducted by experts in their respective fields. Every effort is made to develop the child to the fullest through academic, non-academic and extra curricular activities.



The School has so far Board Examination for classes X & XII respectively and students have been consistently achieving excellent results.



Bangaldesh School Bahrain its effort towards providing excellent, education to those who enter its portals and strives relentlessly towards making the BEST BETTER.
